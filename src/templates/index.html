What is airfree doing, Breaking frontiers in wireless solutions

Airfree platform is an advanced Point-to-multi-point broadband wireless access solution delivering MPLS connectivity for high-speed IP data and Internet applications. The platform's Carrier Class Services at upstream and downstream rates of up to 50Mbps per CPE enables it to address clients' requirement for high bandwidth connectivity solutions.
